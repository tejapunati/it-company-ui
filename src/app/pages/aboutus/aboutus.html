Our Story

Founded in 2010, SSRM Tech Solution began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between exceptional talent and companies seeking innovation. What started as a small team of passionate recruiters has grown into a comprehensive staffing solution provider trusted by businesses of all sizes.

Our journey has been defined by our commitment to understanding both client needs and candidate aspirations. We believe that the right match creates transformative opportunities for both parties, driving growth and innovation across industries.

Our Approach

At SSRM Tech Solution, we believe in a consultative approach to staffing. We take the time to understand not just the technical requirements of a role, but also the company culture, team dynamics, and long-term objectives.

Our process involves thorough screening, technical assessments, and personality evaluations to ensure we present only the most qualified candidates who will thrive in your environment.

  • Comprehensive needs assessment
  • Rigorous candidate screening
  • Cultural fit evaluation
  • Ongoing support and follow-up
